This chapter includes a previously published co-authored paper ~\cite{2024_dark_mesons}, with credit belonging to the ATLAS collaboration.
The author of this thesis contributed to the analysis in multiple areas including a truth study of the multi-lepton channel, verifying preselection yields for the all-hadronic channel, verifying the 4-d ABCD method for the all-hadronic channel, and assisting in the development of the analysis and plotting frameworks for the one-lepton channel.
After the paper in sections \ref{sec:dark_meson_intro}--\ref{sec:dark_meson_conclusion}, additional details on the multi-lepton channel truth study, preselection yields, and the 4-d ABCD method are included in sections \ref{sec:multi_lepton_truth_study}, \ref{sec:allhad_preselection_yields}, and \ref{sec:allhad_abcd_method}, respectively.
