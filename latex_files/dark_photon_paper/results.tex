
Since no significant excess over the SM prediction is observed, upper limits on the branching ratio of the exotic decay $H\to\gamma\gamma_d$ are set using the profile likelihood ratio test statistic and the CL$_\text{s}$ approach \cite{Read:2002hq}, in the asymptotic approximation \cite{Cowan:2010js}. An observed (expected) upper limit on \br\ of $1.16\%$ ($0.92^{+0.46}_{-0.28}\%$) is obtained at 95\% CL.
