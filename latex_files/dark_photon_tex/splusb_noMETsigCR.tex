In this section, the blind signal+background fit is shown in a fit configuration without the low \ETmiss significance region as a CR. In this configuration, there is no region dedicated to constrain specifically the \jfakey\ background. This results in possible degeneracy with the signal strength and $W\gamma$ or $Z\gamma$ backgrounds. As expected, compared to the nominal fit, the background expectation after the fit remains perfectly consistent, but the NP associated to the \jfakey\ systematic uncertainty is less constrained and larger correlations are observed with the signal strength and the $Z\gamma$ or $W\gamma$ normalization factors, as shown in the ranking plot and correlation matrix in figures \ref{fig:rankingplot_nocr} and \ref{fig:corr_splusb_nocr}. 
Figure~\ref{fig:pre_post_plots_nocr} shows background and signal distributions before and after the fit in SR and in CRs, and the corresponding event yields are summarized in Tables~\ref{tab:yields_pre_syst_nocr} and~\ref{tab:yields_post_syst_nocr}. 
The fitted values of the k-factors and their related statistical uncertainties are shown in Figure~\ref{fig:kfactors_splusb_nocr}.
