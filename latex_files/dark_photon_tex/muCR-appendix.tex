In the muon CRs, the \ETmiss is recomputed treating muons as invisible particles to mimick the kinematics of the SR. The distributions of the variables entering the SR definition are compared for $W\gamma$ events in SR and 1mu CR in Figure \ref{fig:cr_sr_comp_wg}, and for $Z\gamma$ events in SR and 2mu CR in Figure \ref{fig:cr_sr_comp_zg}. Good agreement is observed in both cases.
