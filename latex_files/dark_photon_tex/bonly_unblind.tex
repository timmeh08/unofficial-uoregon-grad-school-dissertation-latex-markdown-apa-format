The background-only fit is performed simultaneously on data in the CRs and SR. The signal strength parameter is not included in this fit. The results are, as expected, consistent with the signal+background fit in the background only hypothesis, shown in section \ref{sec:splusb_unblind}. 
The plots in figure \ref{fig:regions_jy_ey_Fit_syst_bonly_unblind} show the post-fit $m_T$ distributions in SR, low \ETmiss sig. CR, 1$\mu$CR and 2$\mu$CR. The values of the k-factors and their related statistical uncertainties are shown in figure \ref{fig:kfactors_syst_bonly_unblind}.\\
Finally, figures \ref{fig:pullplot_syst_bonly_unblind}, \ref{fig:corr_syst_bonly_unblind} and \ref{fig:rankingplot_bonly_unblind} display the pull plots for systematics and MC statistical nuisance parameters, the correlation matrix and the ranked impacts.
